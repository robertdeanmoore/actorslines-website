---
title: Theming a play from its cover
summary: Turn a play's cover photo into a colour theme for its scene list, Settings and more.
date: 2026-07-08
---

When you position a play's cover image, you can check "Use this as a theme?" to pull an accent
colour straight out of the photo — the same idea Android uses to theme your home screen from
your wallpaper, just seeded from the cover instead.

## Where it shows

- A play's own scene list (Play Setup) always uses that play's own theme, if it opted in.
- The Play List screen, Settings and the Font picker all use whichever play is first in your
  list — not a search through every play, just the first one.

> **Tip:** If the first play in your list hasn't checked the box, those three screens simply
> keep the app's normal look. Leaving every play unchecked keeps the app exactly as it is
> today.

## What stays the same

Scene Editor, Practice, Rehearse, Teach and the other line screens keep their own reading-paper
look regardless — this only affects the surrounding app chrome.

## Changing or removing it

- Re-crop the same cover or replace it with a new photo — the checkbox keeps its state either
  way, so a new photo simply becomes the new theme source.
- Uncheck the box, or tap the cover icon and choose "Remove image", to drop back to the default
  look.
