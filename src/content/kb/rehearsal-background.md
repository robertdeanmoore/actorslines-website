---
title: Rehearsing with the screen locked
summary: A Rehearsal run keeps listening and speaking even if you lock your phone mid-run.
date: 2026-07-08
---

Once a Rehearsal run is under way, locking your phone (or it locking itself, e.g. in your
pocket) doesn't stop the run. Cue lines keep speaking, your lines keep being scored, and
pausing/resuming by voice keeps working, all with the screen off.

- You'll see an ongoing notification while a run is live, with the play/scene and your current
  line. It has Pause/Resume buttons you can tap right from the lock screen without unlocking
  your phone.

> **Tip:** If you decline the notification permission, the run still keeps going through a lock
> — you just won't see the notification or its lock-screen controls.

- The notification clears itself as soon as you stop the run or it completes.
