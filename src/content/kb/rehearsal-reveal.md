---
title: Rehearsal's line-by-line reveal
summary: Other characters' lines stay hidden until the run reaches them, so nothing tips you off early.
date: 2026-07-08
---

Before you press Run, Rehearsal shows every line, with everything but your chosen starting
point dimmed — a quiet preview of the scene.

- Once the run starts, lines ahead of the one currently playing (by TTS or a Selftape
  recording) disappear completely — no speaker name, no text — so you're not prompted about
  what's coming.
- Each line is revealed in full colour the moment the run reaches it, and stays that way for
  the rest of the run.

> **Tip:** Pausing doesn't hide anything back down, and it doesn't reveal what's still ahead
> either — resume right where you left off.

- Your own lines are unaffected by this — they already stay hidden until you read them (or
  tap to peek while paused), exactly as before.
