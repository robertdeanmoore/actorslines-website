Welcome! This walkthrough takes you from installing the app to running your first
scene with a synthetic cast.

## 1. Import your script

Open the app, tap **Import**, and either scan your paper script with the camera or
pick a PDF. Actors Lines reads it **on your device** — nothing is uploaded — and
builds your scenes, characters and lines automatically. Review the result and fix
any lines the scanner misread.

## 2. Tell it who you are

Pick your character. Everything the app does — hiding lines, scoring accuracy,
choosing what to speak aloud — revolves around which part is yours.

## 3. Choose voices for the rest of the cast

Every other character gets a synthesised voice. Mix and match until the cast feels
right; you can even blend voices to create new ones.

## 4. Run the scene

Start with **Practice** to read along and hide your lines as you learn them, then
graduate to **Rehearse**: the app speaks everyone else's lines and listens to yours,
scoring your accuracy line by line.

> Tip: the accuracy heat-map on each play card shows exactly where you need work.
