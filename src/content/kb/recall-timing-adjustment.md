---
title: Recall Timing Adjustment
summary: Give yourself more (or less) time to recall your own lines in Practice, as a single global slider.
date: 2026-07-14
---

When you are first learning your lines, you may want to give yourself a few more seconds to
remember the line each time before the app moves on. As you get better, you may want more of a
speed run. Use this to shorten or increase the countdown for your lines (0 is the speed you
recorded/prepared at).

It sits right under Pace in Settings, ranges from -50% to +50% in 5% steps, and defaults to 0%.
It scales the stored countdown for every "my line" prompt in Practice — at +50% a 10-second
countdown becomes 15 seconds; at -20% it becomes 8.

> **Tip:** While this slider is anywhere other than 0%, the small floating +/- buttons that
> normally let you nudge a single line's countdown live don't appear — they'd be adjusting an
> already globally-scaled value, which would only be confusing. Set it back to 0% (it snaps
> there easily, right in the middle) to get them back.

> **Tip:** Only affects Practice's own-line countdown — it doesn't change Pace between other
> characters' cue lines, or anything in Rehearsal or Teach.
